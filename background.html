Background

The Same Daughter is a mystery comic, and the following is a brief synopsis of the story:

Family changes and growth environment:Gil So-myeong was originally a model student with excellent grades and good conduct, and has been living a seemingly perfect life according to her mother's requirements. However, her family has suffered one after another, her father disappeared, and her brother drowned, and she suspects that his death is related to her mother.

The complicated relationship between mother and daughter：Mother is a very controlling person, she has strict requirements for her daughter's life and study, hoping to cultivate her into a perfect daughter. In the process of growing up, the daughter gradually realized the repression caused by her mother's control, so she wanted to get rid of her mother's control, and a fierce psychological game began between mother and daughter.

The secret and the truth behind it：With the development of the story, Xiaoming gradually discovers her mother's unknown past and the deeper secrets hidden behind it during the investigation.
